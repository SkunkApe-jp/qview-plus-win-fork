## ✨ What's New in qView Plus?

### 🤖 Advanced AI Suite
Transform how you interact with your images using our integrated AI tools:
- **AI Q&A Chatbot**: A dedicated chat interface to ask questions about your images. Supports full **Markdown** and **LaTeX Math** rendering.
- **AI Rename**: Instantly generate descriptive, intelligent filenames with a single click or by pressing **`R`**.
- **AI Description**: Get comprehensive, detailed descriptions of any image, rendered in beautiful rich text.
- **AI OCR (Text Extraction)**: Extract text from images with high accuracy and preserve formatting.

### 🖼️ Modern Gallery Sidebar
- **Instant Access**: A sleek, dockable sidebar for browsing images in your folder.
- **Zero Clutter**: Scroll through thumbnails without distracting scrollbars.
- **Hotkey Driven**: Toggle the sidebar instantly with **`G`** or the hamburger menu.

### 🎨 Refined minimalist UI
- **Native Look**: Clean, professional styling that matches your system.
- **High-Contrast Design**: Lighter text and subtle hover effects for maximum legibility.
- **Holiday Theme**: Enjoy our exclusive glassmorphic icon with a festive Santa hat! 🎄
